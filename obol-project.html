Obol

Welcome to the Obol Portfolio, a showcase of the design journey that brought to life the revolutionary E-wallet, empowering users to explore the world of sustainable investments and forge meaningful connections between small merchants and conscious consumers.

Obol's mission was to bridge the gap between consumers and merchants in the financial market while promoting financial literacy among young adults. The challenge was to create a user-centric application that offered a low-risk platform for exploring investments in small businesses and government funds.

As a sole designer and innovator behind Obol, I undertook multiple roles, including researcher, user tester, UI/UX designer, product designer, and prototype crafter. It allowed me to fully immerse myself in the creative process, ensuring a seamless and holistic user experience.
